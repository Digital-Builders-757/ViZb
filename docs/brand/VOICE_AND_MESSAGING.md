# Voice and messaging — ViBE

**Last updated:** March 23, 2026

**Authority:** Expand only in alignment with **`docs/BRAND_SYSTEM.md`** §11 (Voice & Tone). This file is the **agent-friendly** summary.

## Voice pillars

| Pillar | Do | Don’t |
|--------|-----|--------|
| **Bold** | Confident CTAs, clear stakes | Corporate filler (“leverage synergy”) |
| **Inclusive** | Welcome DMV creatives, varied scenes | Exclusive or gatekeepy slang |
| **Authentic** | Real venues, real photography credits | Stock-photo bland |
| **Editorial** | Punchy headlines, rhythm | Long passive paragraphs on marketing surfaces |

## Product vocabulary (prefer)

- **Event**, **RSVP**, **ticket**, **organizer**, **lineup**, **flyer**, **door / check-in** (when built).

## In-app vs marketing

- **Marketing:** more expressive, neon moments allowed.
- **Dashboards:** still ViBE, but prioritize **clarity and density** — mono labels, sharp grids (`BRAND_SYSTEM` dashboard section).
